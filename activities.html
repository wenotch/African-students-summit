ACTIVITIES

The movement Achieves its goal by organizing: public lectures and rallies to encourage the public to appreciate home made goods; competitions to encourage socio-economic ingenuity among students; campaigns against social vice s amongst others.

Our Activities

Ambassadoral Training

This is a specialized skill acquisition open to the public, with resource persons who are experts in their fields. It requires publicity and enormous preparations, to train the students in various fields. This could be organized once a semester or more, depending on logistics.

Motivational Meeting

This is the weekly meeting off the association. There is a list of topics to be addressed at each meeting, invitation is extended to students. Members of the induction crew organize and ensure that motivational meetings are orderly and new members are given extra briefing and follow up

Business Meeting

This is a meeting exclusive to only registered members, where activities of the movement is planed ad issues affecting the association are discussed. Regular business meetings hold at least once a month.

Road Show

This activity is aimed at advertising the association and producers. It is organised at least once a semester. Banners and handbills are displayed and distributed during this activity.

Road walk

This is a fitness activity, where we meet to exercise and keep fit. It is used also a means for publicity for the association and producers product.

Physical Exercise

This is a fitness activity, where we meet to exercise and keep fit. It is used also a means for publicity for the association and producers product.
