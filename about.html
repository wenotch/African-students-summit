ABOUT US

African Socioeconomic Summit is a non-profit making non-governmental organisation seeking to recreate the African mindset to a culture of socioeconomic freedom. Self-freedom starts with the right mindset and continuous to the right action.

JOIN

Criticising the Western world for impoverishing Africa or the home Government for perpetuating corruption is not enough. The beggerly culture and consumerism attitude must give way to a positive mind that creates wealth, appreciates homemade goods and strives to celebrate African dynamic creativity!!! Objectives of the Summit are to encourage Africans to:

  • Appreciate and patronize homemade and indigenous goods;
  • Appreciate and sustain African virtues and values;
  • Love fellow Africans and appreciate African socioeconomic initiatives;

FOCUS

Economic Focus

Production & Recycling
Marketing & Advertisement
Consumption & Preservation

Social Focus

Redefining Culture: Reaffirming cultural purity for self reliance; names, dresses, homemade, Cultural deposits for socioeconomic creativity; Redefining Religion: Free world to adopt any religion and indigenize your religion

Freedom is our goal

Political Independence without socioeconomic independence is no independence because the piper dictates the tune.

TRIPOD STRUCTURE

Ideology

If the citizens are gainfully employed the overdependence on Government will reduce; If the citizens are gainfully employed the idea of ethnic cleansing will be reduced because people will be very busy; If the citizens are gainfully employed overdependence on religious and fraudulent gangsters will reduce; If the citizens are convinced that there is something good in their ethnic origin and that they can be more creative than others, they will challenge any external racial subjugation.

Advocacy

Government Liaising with Government agencies to create conducive environment for socioeconomic wellbeing of the people; Government to establish Industrial sites, warehouses, research on preservation of economic produce, as well as encouraging the economic principle of comparative advantage within multiethnic nations. Advertising­ – Blowing African Trumpet Bring to the awareness of the public some very good home made goods Legal Services African Socioeconomic Summit provides legal services to Cooperative Societies of the Summit in moments of internal rancor and litigations. International Human Rights Commission and Activists are consulted where necessary. General Re-orientation Creating awareness on the evil of overdependence on exotic products, whether social or economic
