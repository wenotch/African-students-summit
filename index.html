PHILOSOPHY

Mental slavery is the highest form of self-humiliation and servitude. it makes one to consider onseself as pertually inferior to the other. It makes one to see one's incapacitation and limitations as against ones potentialities and prowess. Get a youth gainfully employed and he will not nurse the idea of social vices.
